baking intructionss

  1. Flour: All-purpose flour is what I used to develop the recipe.
  2. Leavening: Baking powder is.< my choice of leavening for this muffin base.
  3. Salt: Because no recipe is edible without a pinch or two of salt.
  4. Butter: I prefer unsalted sweet cream butter when making muffins.
  5. Sugar: Only a little sugar is used.
  6. Eggs: You need 2 large eggs.
